What does it mean to target data scientists?

All our ads are targeted by content and geography. For data science, we classify our publishers and individual pages on publisher sites and only show ads next to content related to data science and machine learning.

Will my ads be blocked?

We are part of the acceptable ads program which allows our ads to be shown through many ad blockers. You only pay when your ad is shown. You never pay for blocked ads.
