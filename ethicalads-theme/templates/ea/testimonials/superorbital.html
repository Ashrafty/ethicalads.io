About SuperOrbital

Kubernetes is extremely complicated and it usually isn't a development team's core competency. They're trying to stay focused on shipping features and building their product rather than fighting with their infrastructure. SuperOrbital helps development teams tackle Kubernetes through engineering and training. They teach teams how to use the platform, and jump in to help out during implementation.

EthicalAds was a hyper focused channel to find engineers struggling with their infrastructure and guide them toward SuperOrbital's workshops. Promoting solutions to DevOps problems right next to to the relevant projects and documentation ended up being a fantastic way to fill their workshop pipeline.
