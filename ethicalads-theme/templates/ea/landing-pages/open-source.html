Transparent and Open

The code behind most other ad networks is proprietary and complex. Instead, we are completely transparent in how our advertising works.

No Cookies

We don't use cookies to track users, but we match ads based on the content of your site. This gives your users ultimate privacy and relevant ads, the best of both worlds.

Invested in Open Source

EthicalAds is itself open source software. We're the only open source ad network on the market. And we're growing.

Open source projects use EthicalAds

EthicalAds came from our work monetizing Read the Docs, an open source documentation building and hosting platform. We wanted an open, transparent ad network that respected privacy, but there wasn't an ad network out there willing to work with us on those terms (we looked). Instead, we built our own and it's used by lots of open source projects and sites.
