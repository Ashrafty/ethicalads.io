Battle-tested technology

Our sponsorship platform is built on the same open source code that powers millions of views each day on our ad network. Using the same technology we can ensure your sponsorships load reliably and incredibly fast. On top of that, we provide:

  • Weighted display based on sponsorship level (larger sponsors are shown more frequently)
  • Automatically target your sponsorships to the most relevent content for your sponsors
  • Syncronize your Open Collective sponsors, so your ad rotation is always up to date with your sponsor tiers

How much does it cost?

During our private beta, we have pricing of $.025 CPM, or $25 per 1 million sponsorship requests. There is a minimum cost of $10/mo, which you will pay if you have fewer than 400,000 requests. A sponsorship request happens every time a page loads and you request a sponsorship be shown.
